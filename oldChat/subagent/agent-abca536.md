### 3. How to handle the Essay 3 punchline specifically

As detailed above: name the topic (absurdity), name the examples (Sisyphus, office worker, cat), name the method (progression from mythic to mundane), but do NOT name the outcome (absurdity dissolves) or the mechanism (internally generated meaning). Say "the final example produces a recognition the reader must encounter firsthand." This is the classical Chinese approach — the 论语 never tells you what you should feel when reading a passage; it presents the situation and trusts the reader. We should do the same.
