## Claude at 2026-02-11 03:57:02

Analysis sent to team-lead. Key findings:

1. **感受 appears across all essays** with a consistent meaning: any constituent element of the phenomenal field. The author explicitly defines it in Essay 6 line 166 as interchangeable with 体验, referring to "any part of P [phenomena]."

2. **Critical distinction: 感受 vs. 经验.** The author reserves 经验 for the epistemological/empirical sense (section titles: 经验的总结, 经验的预测, 经验的抽象). This distinction must be preserved in translation.

3. **The author distinguishes two senses of 感受** in Essay 5 fn2: (1) received sensation; (2) the structured "crystallization/surging/turbulence" — with sense 2 being the primary meaning.

4. **My recommendation: "felt experience"** as the primary rendering, with "feeling" permitted in affective contexts, and "experience" reserved for 经验. This preserves the terminological architecture of the original.
