### Insights Worth Remembering
- The cat example is philosophically elegant — dissolves absurdism by making you *notice* you never felt absurd
- The 道德仁義禮 "inversion" may be epistemologically right but ontologically contestable
- Author does philosophy *with* Chinese materials, not *within* Chinese traditions
- Translation must preserve experiential sequences — they're functional experiments
