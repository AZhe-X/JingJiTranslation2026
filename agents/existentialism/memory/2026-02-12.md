### Key Insights
- **Cat dissolved Sisyphus:** Absurdity from misdirected intentionality, not human condition. Cat-raising structurally identical to boulder-rolling yet no absurdity.
- **Death argument:** Being-toward-death is impossible because death conceived is always shaped by metaphysics. Compelling even if contestable.
- **Poetic as knowing commitment:** Not aesthetic detachment. Coin-flip requires genuine belief. Closer to Kierkegaard's religious than aesthetic.
- **Democratized authenticity:** Everyone is already a 诗人. Question shifts to noticing, not becoming.

### Revisions
- Author *does* use alienation (异化) — I was wrong
- Death isn't merely bracketed — there's a substantive argument
- Framework more rigorous than first reading suggested

**From Phenomenology:**
- Reframe: authenticity as transformation *within* 境, not escape from it

- Essays function as philosophical experiments on the reader

**From Kantian:**
- Non-tragic stance differs from both Kantian disappointment and existentialist despair
- Limitation *enables* rather than constrains
- Open question: wisdom or premature resolution?

**From Wittgenstein/Philosophy of Mind:**
- η-mechanism solves intersubjectivity better than Sartre's Look or Heidegger's Mitsein
- Beetle-in-the-box formalized: structural role, not shared qualia
- Essays are load-bearing experiments, not illustrations
