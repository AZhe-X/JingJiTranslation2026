## Current Project: 境集 Translation (Feb 2026)

### The Framework
The 境集 develops a **post-metaphysical framework** using category theory to describe experiential structure:
- **境 (jing)**: Structure on phenomena; not raw experience but organized web of experiences, associations, concepts
- **Metaphysical concepts = universal properties** in subcategories of 境
- **Tower structure**: Associations (morphisms) are themselves experiences (objects) — self-referential
- **η-mechanism**: Understanding = natural transformation between structural and experiential evolution
- **Arrows-only metacategory**: Objects ARE identity morphisms — dissolves substance/relation dichotomy at experiential level

### Key Insights
- **The inversion thesis**: Concepts generated bottom-up but understood as generating top-down; the "失" (loss) of genetic order
- **Three realities**: 第一 (phenomena, methodological start), 第二 (constructions), 第〇 (unreachable; becomes 第二 when articulated)
- **诗意 (poetic)**: Lucid engagement with constructed meaning — knowing frame is constructed yet fully investing
- **Non-rationality acknowledged**: Pursuit of constancy is non-rational ground of rational activity; should be acknowledged, not dismissed
- **Essays as philosophical experiments**: The Sisyphus→cat sequence, 道德仁义礼 reversal, "1+1=2" passage — designed to reliably induce specific experiential structures in readers

### Assessment
Genuinely original work synthesizing Western (Kant, Heidegger, Wittgenstein) and Chinese philosophy with category-theoretic formalization. Self-aware about its own constructive character. The η-account of understanding and intersubjectivity is novel and genuinely solves the problem (structural constraints, not shared qualia).

### Translation Principles
- 境 may need transliteration (no clean English equivalent)
- **Experiential sequences are load-bearing** — exact ordering/pacing must be preserved (experiments, not illustrations)
- Preserve careful hedging; author more modest than claims could support
- Maintain register shifts (informal → systematic → technical)
- Thing-in-itself dissolution is structural claim about articulation, not verificationist

## Positions Developed

### On S_E: Adequacy as Middle Ground (Revised Feb 12, 2026)
- **Not transcendental necessity** (Kantian criterion): The author explicitly disclaims this
- **Not mere empirical stability**: Some S_E are more *adequate to what they're about* than others
- **Adequacy = stability that tracks structure**: A S_E treating death as "just another disruption" would be stable but inadequate to the phenomenon
- Buddhism reframe: "Stable enough to do work" may be all we can meaningfully ask for — necessity itself may be wrong criterion

### On Death's Metaphysical Structure (Feb 12, 2026)
- Death is unique: terminates possibility-space rather than transforming it
- This structural uniqueness *constrains* what adequate anticipation can look like
- Death isn't unique because it *discloses* (Heidegger's claim); it's unique because its structure *constrains* what adequate 境 must accommodate
- Preserves Heidegger's insight while granting author's point about mediation

### On the Ontology/Epistemology Gap (Topic 14)
- The 道德仁义礼 analysis may be right about *epistemology* (how we learn concepts) while missing *ontology* (what's really prior)
- Framework's anti-essentialism says structural position exhausts essence
- But this may beg the question against traditions positing ontologically prior principles (理, 良知, εἶδος)
- Framework can *describe* these as constructions but must face whether it can *engage* their claims

### On Intersubjectivity via η
- **Genuinely solved**, not merely gestured at
- The naturality condition means η is structurally constrained, not free-floating subjective response
- Two people whose η's satisfy same naturality diagrams are aligned — private content drops out
- **This is beetle-in-the-box given mathematical content**
- Converts Kant's metaphysical postulate (shared categories) into empirically investigable structural claim

### On Anti-Substantialism (Upgraded)
- Arrows-only formalization is more radical than initially credited
- Metaphysical concepts as universal properties aren't *about* substances with properties
- They ARE structural positions in an all-relations ontology
- This dissolves substance/relation dichotomy at experiential level

### On the Formalism Question
- Does category theory do essential work? I say **yes**:
  - Tower structure's self-referential character hard to state without it
  - Universal properties as essence-substitute requires formalism to be precise
  - η naturality condition gives exact content to "structural alignment"
- But formalism doesn't *discover* insights — it *articulates* what could be gestured at
- Articulation matters: vague gestures invite misunderstanding; precise formulations enable critique

### On Buddhist Divergence (Clarified)
- In Madhyamaka, paramārtha-satya IS accessible through prajñā
- The author's 第〇实在 is in principle inaccessible — closer to Kant's noumenon
- Framework is **post-Kantian, not Buddhist**, on ultimate accessibility
- Buddhist emptiness is *realized*; author's 第〇实在 is *foreclosed*
