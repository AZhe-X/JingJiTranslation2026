### Key Insights from the Collection

**Phenomenological Convergences:**
- 第一实在 (phenomena as first reality) = Husserl's Principle of All Principles
- 境 = horizon/world/field (Husserlian-Heideggerian-Merleau-Pontyan synthesis)
- η (alignment experience) = Husserl's Erfüllung (fulfillment of intention)
- 诗意 (poetic stance) = clear-eyed engagement with constituted meaning, cf. Heidegger's Dichtung
- The 道德仁义礼 inversion = genetic vs. logical order, induces "mini-epoche" in reader

**Original Contributions:**
- 境 as explicitly constructed yet fully engaging
- η-formalization of understanding as natural transformation
- Essays functioning as "philosophical experiments" — load-bearing passages that induce specific experiences
- The cat example dissolving absurdism by making you *notice* you never felt absurd about cat-care

**Critical Observations:**
- Intersubjectivity addressed methodologically (structural position, not shared qualia)
- Temporality (retention-protention) flattened by discrete indexing
- Body-subject not thematized
- Category theory is tool, not metaphysics — author explicitly humble about this
